First Recorded Music

What is contained here are the very first ever computer / MIDI recordings I ever did. I don't know how I have them (although thinking about it, I think I burnt them to a CD to take home). They were created at something I attended called MAP (multi-arts project) and they had a set up a that used a laptop with Reason 3 (I think?). And once I was shown the basics, I started to make stuff. None of it good, however. Although at the time I was probably proud of it. One of them (Harmonix) was used as part of a film we made (Saturday the 14th - original name I know). The other potentially was made WITH someone who just pressed random keys and I worked around it.

These tracks have never been released, they have never been edited, and for many years not even played. They could be called at best experimental.

They have been run through an EQ and things just to clean them up, improve volume and things.

What is continued with the PSS-270 started here I guess. Enjoy?
